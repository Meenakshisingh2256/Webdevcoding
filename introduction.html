Campustay's Key Features and Benefits

Do more each day, automate time-consuming tasks, and get things done with the intelligent feature set of Campustay’s hostel management software:

Personal Dashboard

The personal dashboard provides you with a comprehensive overview of your hostel stay. You can easily view your room details, stay duration, payment history, and other important information. It serves as your one-stop platform to manage and track all hostel-related activities and updates.

Fee Payment Options

Our online fee payment system offers a seamless and secure way to pay your hostel fees. You can view your current and past payment details, check for any pending dues, and pay directly through the website using a variety of payment methods. This feature ensures you never miss a due date and can complete transactions with ease.

Housekeeping

Keeping your living space clean and comfortable is a priority. With the housekeeping feature, you can request services like room cleaning, maintenance, or special requests, all through a simple interface. You can schedule these services based on your convenience, ensuring a hassle-free living experience.

Amenities

We offer several essential amenities, including a fully equipped gym and laundry services, to make your stay more comfortable. You can easily book time slots for the gym or request laundry services directly from the website, allowing you to enjoy these facilities with minimal effort.

Support

If you face any issues or have questions, our support system is available to assist you. Whether it’s a technical issue, a maintenance request, or general queries about hostel facilities, the support feature ensures you can get timely help. You can reach our support team via chat, email, or call.

Visitor Management

For safety and convenience, the visitor management feature allows you to register and track visitors. You can add details of your visitors, schedule their entry, and receive updates on their visit. This helps maintain a secure and organized environment for all residents
